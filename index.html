Straight answers

Questions parents actually ask

Is my child safe from strangers?

Completely. There is no open chat, no DMs, and no way for a stranger to reach your child — ever. Friends only connect when both parents approve. Safety here isn’t a setting you switch on; it’s the way the whole thing is built.

Wait — isn’t this just more screen time?

It’s the opposite, on purpose. Every session ends by sending your kid off the screen to do something real, and their island only grows once you confirm they did it. The app is the nudge. Real life is the game.

What do I pay — and can my kid spend money?

$10 today holds your Founding Family spot and is credited to your first year (refundable anytime, no questions). Founding families lock $49/year for life. Kids can never spend a cent — treasure coins are earned by real-world quests only. No loot boxes, nothing to buy.

When does it launch?

The island is playable right now, free, in your browser — no download, no account. Family subscriptions (parent dashboard, multiple explorers, the Living Storybook) begin soon; founding families lock their price before that happens.

Who’s behind it?

A dad and his 12-year-old daughter, Bianca, who wrote the story it all grew from. We’re building the thing we wanted for our own family.
