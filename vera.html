Field guide

How your island works

1

No blank islands. It appears once it sees you — three questions, and the first pieces it plants are yours forever. No take-backs. The island keeps its first memories of you.

2

Every piece means something. Groves are generosity, watchfires are courage, kites are imagination. Plant things, and the island reads you back: "you are growing joyful, with a streak of brave." It's a mirror, not a sandbox.

3

Storms come. Around every six pieces, the island tests you — the sky darkens and something breaks. Tap the gray pieces to mend them. They come back veined with gold, and gold counts double. Where it broke is where it shines.

4

Surviving storms grows the island. New land rises after every one. That's the only way it expands — no storm, no territory.

5

Glowing things are dares. After a storm, something mysterious washes ashore. It only opens for a REAL-world deed — a real laugh, a real brave thing. Do it, come back, tap it… and what hatches is alive. You can't tap your way to those.

6

Share = introduce yourself. The share button copies a link that IS your island. No scores, no leaderboards. Showing someone your island means showing them you.
