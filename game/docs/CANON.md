# Book canon — THE LITTLE BIG ISLAND by Bianca Lang
(Full manuscript received from the founder 2026-07-12 — editor's pass.
This file is the extraction the game builds from. The book always wins.)

## The journey = the island's geography (in story order)
1. **The Shore & the Sign** — fuzzy island, "WELCOME! NO REFUNDS. ALSO, NO NEED."
   Dragon poop nearby. The sign REWRITES itself at the end (epilogue list of treasures).
2. **The Seashell Path** — a string of shells that "definitely wasn't there a second ago."
3. **The Cave** (Ch1, THE SPARK) — "teeth of rock, breath of coal"; closes on you unless
   you find the Spark: a pinprick of light, one blink from gone — the pocket sunrise.
   Caves don't eat people; they clear their throats.
4. **The Cliff that was a beach** (Ch2, LAUGHTER LIFT) — water flashing silver below.
   Real shared laughter (not show-off joy) grows the BUBBLE VEHICLE. Bragging pops it.
5. **The Laughing Forest** (Ch3, THE HORN + THE NOTE) — Kazoo's horn makes trees dance,
   Boogie Birds, the Singing Sun. The silver NOTE lands on YOUR hand.
6. **The Cracked Garden** (Ch4, THE SEED) — dark, cracked ground; Patch plants seeds;
   soft serious rain; sprouts→leaves→plants. "Even cracked ground wants to bloom."
7. **The Whisper Woods** (Ch5) — pretend-wrong forest, fake collapsing monster,
   whispers ("You can't do it") answered with "WE SEE YOU. STOP BEING DRAMATIC!"
   Secret whisper only YOU hear: "There are more islands than you have shouts."
8. **The Mountain** (Ch6, THE WING) — vertical "nope", clouds give it high-fives;
   GRIT yells BEHOLD! from the top; his wings = the treasure; a lake below he missed.
9. **The Rising Cliff** (Ch7, THE SHIELD) — the ground itself rises; Shaky appears
   (six of you now); the huge shield falls from the sky, has a BUTTON that shrinks it;
   Patch feeds the stray cat, translates its meows.
10. **The Storm / The Gathering** (Ch8) — the island gets ANGRY. Shaky rescues the cat,
    shaking the whole time ("that's what made it brave"). ALL treasures combine:
    seed=shelter+food, wing=steadiness, shield=protection, horn blasts thunder back,
    spark=light. "Treasures work best together."
11. **THE THREAD** (Ch9) — part lighthouse, part treehouse, stitched with light and
    branches; windows of soft gold; smells like roasted marshmallows and Christmastime;
    a library with bean bags. Thread names what each treasure IS (the part of you that…).
    The six weave into a single golden thread → the seventh treasure: FAITH,
    small, mustard-colored, glowing with quiet strength.
12. **The Mirror Lake** (Ch10) — shows not your reflection but your FULL self.
    The companions melt inward: they were pieces of you all along.
    "Made with treasures. Made with purpose. Made by God, little and big all at once."

## The seven treasures (sign order) and what they are
spark=hope/believing before seeing · note=imagination · horn=joy · seed=giving/kindness
· wing=remembering you can · shield=courage/moving while afraid · **thread=FAITH, ties all**

## The companions, per the book (⚠ = differs from current game/painted art)
- **Bright** — man, blue T-shirt, professional guide/amateur disaster, sarcastic, bows
  too deeply, fanny pack about to explode, = your HOPE.
- **Kazoo** — girl, party hat, sings hello, horn, investigation face, = your JOY.
- **Patch** ⚠ — a YOUNG BOY with crumbs and seeds in his hand (not an adult gardener),
  offers raisins ("dried disappointment"), feeds/translates the cat, = your LOVE.
- **Grit** — showoff on the mountaintop, BEHOLD!, owns the wings, = your CONFIDENCE.
- **Shaky** ⚠ — a boy who looks ~8 but is older, stutters ("S-s-sorry"), vibrates,
  rescues the cat in the storm, = your COURAGE. (Knight armor is a game invention.)
- **Thread** ⚠ — a GIRL with a soft, sophisticated voice, keeper of the library
  (not an old woman), speaks in near-rhyme, = your FAITH.

## Unbreakable lines & lore
- "You only get to see the island if it gets to see you." (fog-of-discovery)
- "NO REFUNDS. ALSO, NO NEED."
- The island reacts: scoffs, gets angry, regrets its attitude, "did not care for that."
- Sequel hook: "There are more islands than you have shouts." / "You weren't the
  first. You won't be the last."
- Gentle faith undertone is CANON (Kazoo quotes 1 Samuel 16:7; Faith is the 7th treasure).
